The middle game

The middle game is the phase where players try to create attacking opportunities, control the center, and find combinations to put their opponent in difficulty. To do this, they do:

Exchanges

tactics

coordinated attacks

At the end of the middle game, when the players have massacred each other's pieces, it generally results in a more or less important advantage for one of the two players. He will therefore try to make it happen in what we call the final.

The Finale

The finale is the last phase of the game where the remaining pieces must be used to win the game. Players can achieve this by doing:

A promotion

a take's piece

Checkmate
